At Little Wonders, we believe in nurturing young minds through joyful and engaging learning. Our Montessori and Play-way approach builds confidence, independence, and a love for learning in children aged 2 to 5. Every day is filled with fun, creativity, and discovery in a warm and caring environment.

Fun-Filled Early Learning Approach

Our unique program nurtures young minds through engaging play, storytelling, and hands-on exploration, making learning joyful and effective.

Safe & Supportive Environment

A nurturing space where your child feels secure, valued, and confident to explore.

Daycare Facility Available

Convenient and caring full-day support tailored to working parents' needs.

At Little Wonders, we believe that every child is a unique spark waiting to shine. Our mission is to create a joyful space where learning feels like play, and curiosity is always encouraged. With a blend of proven teaching methods, caring educators, and a safe environment, we are proud to offer children their first steps toward a bright future.

Thank you for trusting us with your child's early journey—we promise to make it magical!

Your Questions,
Answered

What age group do you admit?

We welcome children between 2 to 5 years for Play School, Nursery, LKG, and UKG programs.

What teaching method do you follow?

We follow a blend of Montessori and Play-way methods, focusing on fun, creativity, and learning through hands-on experiences.

Who oversees the school's activities?

All programs are conducted under the expert care of Dr. V.R. Aloke, a leading Neonatologist and Pediatrician with vast experience in child development.
